About Fixtronics

At Fixtronics, we specialize in giving your broken electronic appliances a second life. Our mission is to make electronics repair as hassle-free and accessible as possible...

Our Services

We repair a wide range of electronic appliances, including but not limited to:

  • Refrigerators: Cooling issues, compressor problems, or leaks.
  • Washing Machines: Motor repairs, drainage fixes, and operational faults.
  • Smart TVs: Screen repairs, software troubleshooting, and connectivity issues.
  • Microwave Ovens: Heating problems, electrical faults, and more.
  • Air Conditioners: Maintenance, part replacements, and fault diagnostics.
  • Dishwashers: Pump repairs, door seals, and control panel issues.
